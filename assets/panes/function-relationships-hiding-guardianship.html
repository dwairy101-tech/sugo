Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you prefer not to show your guardianship status to others, you can hide it. Here is how:

  • Go to "Settings" (ME → Settings).
  • Tap on "Privacy Settings".
  • Look for the "Guardianship" or "Display Guardianship" toggle.
  • Turn it OFF.

Once hidden:

  • Other users cannot become your guardian (the "Guard" button will be disabled on your profile).
  • Your guardianship details will not appear in the Guardian Ranking or Guardian CP Ranking.
  • Your existing guardians will still be active, but others will not see the relationship.

To show your guardianship again, simply turn the toggle back ON.

This setting is personal. It does not affect your ability to guard others, only your visibility as a guardian or as someone who can be guarded.

Privacy is important. You control what others see.
