Fast Withdrawal Conditions:

Supported Country: Egypt

Supported Channels: Vodafone and Fawry

Withdrawal Frequency: Once per day

Daily Withdrawal Limit per User: ≤ 10 USD per time

Monthly Total Withdrawal Limit: 300 USD/month

All small withdrawal amounts will be delivered for free within 6 hours.

Withdrawal Requirements:

You must be a host in an agency.

The number of accounts registered on the same device must be 3 or fewer.

The account must not have been logged into on more than 3 devices.

Charm level must be 3.

Users who are flagged as at risk or who do not meet the withdrawal requirements will not be able to perform small withdrawals.
