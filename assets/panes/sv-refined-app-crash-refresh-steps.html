App Crash / Refresh Steps

Answer

Please refresh the app and upload the app log by following these steps:

  1. Go to Settings from the profile/code page.
  2. Select About SUGO.
  3. Tap Upload App Log and wait until the upload is complete.
  4. Close the app completely.
  5. Restart your phone.
  6. Log out of the app, then log in again.

If the issue continues after completing these steps, please send a video showing the message or issue that appears, and make sure your account ID is visible in the video.
