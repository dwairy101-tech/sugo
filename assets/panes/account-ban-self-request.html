English

CS can process a self-ban when the request clearly comes from the same user. For this ban reason, the account can be unblocked later if the user requests unblocking.

  • VIP Level < 6 and Charm Level < 6: confirm through Sobot that the request is from the same user, send a screenshot including the chat content and SUGO ID from Sobot, mention the monitors directly, and monitors can act immediately.
  • VIP Level ≥ 6 and Charm Level < 6: mention the VIP team first for confirmation, then monitors proceed after VIP confirmation.
  • Anchor with Charm Level ≥ 6 and no assigned VIP member: mention the responsible ACM for confirmation. VIP team involvement is not required.
  • Anchor with Charm Level ≥ 6 and an assigned VIP member: mention the responsible VIP first to confirm the request with the anchor. CS should state clearly that the user is an anchor so monitors understand the case. ACM mention is not needed before VIP approval. After VIP approval, monitors proceed with the ban and then inform the ACM in the group. ACM may check with the AGM and negotiate with the anchor if needed, while the action follows the anchor’s own request.
  • For CS: We can ban users if they request to ban themselves, and for those ban reasons we can unblock the accounts if users requests.
  • For users requesting to ban their own accounts:
  • CS confirm the request is from the same user through sobot, send a screenshot including the chat content and Sugo ID from Sobot, mention the monitors directly, and monitors take action immediately.
  • CS mention the VIP team for confirmation first, following the current process, after confirmation from VIP, monitors takes action.
  • Anchors with Charm Level ≥ 6 and no assigned VIP members
  • CS should mention the responsible ACM for confirmation. No need to involve the VIP team.
  • Anchors with Charm Level ≥ 6 and an assigned VIP member
  • CS should mention the responsible VIP to confirm the request with the anchor first. And CS needs to state this user is an anchor to let monitors know, but no need to mention ACM at this stage.
  • After receiving approval from the VIP team, monitors proceed with the ban. After the action is taken, monitors also need to mention the ACM in the group to inform them that the anchor's account has been banned. They can check with the AGM and negotiate with the anchor if needed, but we will take action according to the anchor's willingness under this scenario.
العربية

يمكن لخدمة العملاء تنفيذ حظر ذاتي عندما يكون الطلب واضحًا أنه صادر من نفس المستخدم. في هذا النوع من الحظر، يمكن فك الحظر لاحقًا إذا طلب المستخدم ذلك.

  • مستوى VIP أقل من 6 ومستوى Charm أقل من 6: يتم التأكد عبر Sobot أن الطلب صادر من نفس المستخدم، ثم إرسال لقطة شاشة تتضمن محتوى المحادثة ومعرّف SUGO من Sobot، مع منشن مباشر للمراقبين، ويمكن للمراقبين تنفيذ الإجراء فورًا.
  • مستوى VIP يساوي 6 أو أعلى ومستوى Charm أقل من 6: يتم منشن فريق VIP أولًا للتأكيد، ثم يتابع المراقبون الإجراء بعد موافقة فريق VIP.
  • مضيف بمستوى Charm يساوي 6 أو أعلى ولا يوجد له عضو VIP مسؤول: يتم منشن ACM المسؤول للتأكيد، ولا حاجة لإشراك فريق VIP.
  • مضيف بمستوى Charm يساوي 6 أو أعلى ولديه عضو VIP مسؤول: يتم منشن VIP المسؤول أولًا لتأكيد الطلب مع المضيف. يجب أن يوضح CS أن المستخدم مضيف حتى يفهم المراقبون الحالة. لا حاجة لمنشن ACM قبل موافقة VIP. بعد موافقة VIP ينفذ المراقبون الحظر ثم يبلغون ACM داخل المجموعة. يمكن لـ ACM المتابعة مع AGM والتفاوض مع المضيف إذا لزم، مع تنفيذ الإجراء بناءً على رغبة المضيف نفسه.
