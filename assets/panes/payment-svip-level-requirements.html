Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

SVIP has 7 levels. Each level requires a certain amount of recharge EXP (1 coin = 1 EXP) within a rolling 30-day window.

  • The exact thresholds are displayed in the SVIP Center (ME → SVIP). They may be updated from time to time.
  • Higher levels require significantly more coins. For example, SVIP7 requires millions of coins recharged in 30 days.
  • To maintain your level, you must meet the required EXP each 30-day cycle. If you fall below, you will degrade as explained in the previous section.
  • To upgrade to a higher level, you need to exceed the threshold for that level within the same 30-day period.

Check the SVIP Center regularly to see your current progress and the requirements for the next level.
