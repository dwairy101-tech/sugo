Report Insulting Management

Answer

To report abuse related to management in a private chat or voice room, please send a clear screen recording that includes the following:

  1. Open the SUGO app.
  2. Clearly show the abusive content in the chat or room.
  3. Clearly show the violator's ID.

The report will be forwarded to the relevant department. If the report is confirmed, the appropriate action will be taken according to the approved policies.
