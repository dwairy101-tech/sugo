Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you notice a deduction in your SVIP points, it is most likely due to the expiration of points.

  • SVIP points are valid for 90 days. Points older than 90 days expire automatically.
  • When you redeem items from the point store, the system uses points closest to expiration first. This can also cause a deduction.
  • To see your full points history, go to ME → SVIP → tap the "Record" icon (top right). You will see all additions, redemptions, and expirations.

No points are deducted without a valid reason. Regularly check your point balance and expiration dates to avoid surprises.
