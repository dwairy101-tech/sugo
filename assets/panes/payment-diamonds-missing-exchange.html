Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you do not see the "Exchange" button on your diamonds page, here are the most common reasons:

  • Charm level too low: The exchange feature unlocks at Charm Level 1 or higher. Increase your charm by interacting with others.
  • Negative diamond balance (debt): If you have received diamonds that were later refunded or identified as fraudulent, your balance may be negative. Clear the debt by recharging or earning diamonds.
  • Unsupported country or region: This feature may not be available in your location yet.
  • Account restrictions: In rare cases, accounts with security flags may have this feature disabled.

Once you meet the requirements, the Exchange button will appear automatically.

We once again sincerely apologize for the inconvenience we have caused, and we truly thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a happy and successful day!
