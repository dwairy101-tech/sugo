We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

The cost of sending a message to a female user may vary from one user to another. Here is why:

  • Female users can set their own message price. The default is 160 coins per message.
  • As a female user's Charm Level increases, she unlocks the ability to set higher prices: 200 or 240 coins per message.
  • Therefore, if you message different female users, you may pay different amounts depending on their individual settings.
  • The price is displayed before you send the message (usually next to the send button or in the chat settings).
  • If you find the price too high, you can choose not to message that user or try to become mutual followers (which makes messaging free).

This system allows female users to manage their inbox and rewards higher charm levels. We once again sincerely apologize for the inconvenience we have caused, and we truly thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a happy and successful day!
