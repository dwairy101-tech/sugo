Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

Daily and family tasks have specific requirements:

  1. Complete frame verification and gender verification.
  2. Have a recharge record within the last 30 days.
  3. Account level must be 4 or higher.
  4. Charm level must be 5 or higher.

If the account meets the recharge requirement, the feature may become active. If all requirements are met, the feature may become active the next day.

We once again sincerely apologize for the inconvenience we have caused, and we truly thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a happy and successful day!
