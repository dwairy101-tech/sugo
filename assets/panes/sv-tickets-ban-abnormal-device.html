Abnormal Device

Answer

This notification appears because more than five accounts have been registered on the same device. As a result, the system classifies the device as abnormal.

Accordingly, accounts cannot be used on this device, and new accounts cannot be created through it. We apologize, but there is no available solution for this case according to the approved system policy.
