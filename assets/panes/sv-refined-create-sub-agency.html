Create Sub-Agency

Answer

You can create a sub-agency under a main agency by contacting the main agency agent directly and requesting a sub-agency. The agent will take the required steps according to the approved process.

To create an administrative WhatsApp group, the following requirements must be met:

  1. The agency income must double for two consecutive months.
  2. The agency must achieve 40 million diamonds within 30 days.
  3. Ten new active hosts must be added during the same 30-day period.

After the requirements are achieved, a WhatsApp group with management will be created in the month following the month in which the target was achieved.
