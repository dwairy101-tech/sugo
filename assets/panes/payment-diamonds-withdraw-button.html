Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you believe you are eligible to withdraw diamonds but do not see the Withdraw button, consider these possibilities:

  • You are not a host or agency leader: Only female hosts in agencies and male agency leaders can withdraw.
  • Negative diamond balance (debt): You must clear any negative balance before withdrawal is enabled.
  • Charm or VIP level too low: Some minimum levels may be required. Check your levels.
  • Region not supported: Withdrawal may not be available in your country yet.
  • Previously removed: If you requested to have the withdrawal option removed, it will stay hidden until you submit a request to restore it.
  • Account age or activity: New accounts or inactive accounts may not have withdrawal privileges immediately.

If you meet all criteria and still do not see the button, please provide your account ID for further review.

Best regards,

Sugo Support Team

  • If the "Withdraw" option is not visible, it is likely for one of the following reasons:
  • You are not yet an eligible host. The feature is primarily for agency-affiliated hosts. If you are a female user, please make sure you have successfully joined an agency.
  • You have an account debt. If your account has a negative diamond balance (often due to refunded or fraudulent transactions from other users), the withdrawal option will be hidden until this debt is cleared.
  • Your Charm Level or VIP level is too low. A minimum Charm Level or VIP level is required to unlock the feature.
  • You Previously Requested Its Removal. If you have submitted a request in the past to have the 'Withdraw' button removed from your account, it will remain hidden.
  • To get it back, you must submit a new request using this form: https://micoworld.feishu.cn/share/base/form/shrcnm0SCrveAVtp0l5m25MBF8e
  • Please Note: This form is only for restoring a previously removed feature. It cannot be used to request access if you don't meet the other eligibility requirements. The "Withdraw" button will only appear automatically once your account meets all the necessary criteria.
