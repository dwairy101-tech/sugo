Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If the app asks you to verify your profile or gender repeatedly, here is why:

  • New device login: Your verification status is tied to the device you used when you first verified. If you log in on a new device, you will need to verify again. This is a security measure.
  • App reinstall or data clear: Uninstalling the app or clearing its data removes the local verification token. The app will ask you to verify again.
  • Long inactivity: If you haven't logged in for several months, the system may require re-verification for security.

What to do: Simply complete the verification process again. It only takes a minute.

Prevention: Use the same device for logging in consistently. If you must switch devices, expect to verify again.

This process protects your account from unauthorized access. Thank you for understanding.
