Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you receive a message that you cannot follow more users, you have reached the maximum number of accounts you are allowed to follow.

  • Standard users: Can follow up to 300 accounts.
  • VIP users: Have higher limits. VIP1: up to 1,000; VIP5: up to 1,500; VIP7: up to 2,000.
  • If you have reached your limit, you will need to unfollow some accounts before you can follow new ones.
  • To unfollow, go to the user's profile, tap the "..." menu, and select "Unfollow".
  • Upgrading to a higher VIP level increases your follow limit significantly.

Following too many accounts can make your feed crowded. We recommend following only those you truly interact with.

Thank you for managing your follow list responsibly.

Best regards,
