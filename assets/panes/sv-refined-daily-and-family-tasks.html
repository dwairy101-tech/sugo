Daily and Family Tasks

Answer

There are current requirements for enabling daily and family tasks:

  1. Pass photo/frame verification and gender verification.
  2. Have a recharge record within the last 30 days.
  3. Account level must be 4 or above.
  4. Charm level must be 5 or above.

The feature will be activated once the required conditions are met, and it may appear on the next day after the activation requirements are completed.
