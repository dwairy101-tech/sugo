Report Abuse Case

Answer

To report abuse in a private chat or voice room, please send a clear screen recording that includes the following:

  1. Open the SUGO app.
  2. Open the full chat, or show the evidence related to the abuse inside the voice room.
  3. Open the violator's profile and show their account details, then open and show your own account details as well.
  4. Open Google Chrome or any browser, type the current time, and show it in the video.

After receiving the evidence, the report will be forwarded to the relevant department for review. If the report is confirmed, the appropriate action will be taken according to the app policies.
