Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you are a male user and you send a message to a female user whom you do not mutually follow, coins will be deducted. Here is how it works:

  • Cost: The female user sets her message price (default 160 coins, can be 200 or 240). You pay that amount per message.
  • Refund: If the female user does not reply to your message within 12 hours, the coins will be automatically returned to your account.
  • Mutual follow: Once you and the female user follow each other (mutual follow), messaging becomes free. No coins are deducted.
  • Check if unfollowed: If you notice deductions after you thought you were mutual followers, the other person may have temporarily unfollowed you.
  • No bugs: There are no known bugs with this system. The deduction logic is consistent.

To avoid coin deductions, build mutual follow relationships with the users you chat with frequently.
