English

The weekly room task is a promotional support task for lower-level room owners and is not available to every user.

The task appears only when the room received less than 400,000 diamonds during the previous week and the room owner is not a coin reseller, not an SVIP user, and not an official account owner.

If the room received 400,000 diamonds or more during the previous week, the weekly room task will not appear in the following week.

The previous week’s room diamond income can be checked from Rank → Room Rank → Weekly Rank → Previous Week.

  • Rooms income< 400k diamonds last week

  • The Weekly Room Task is a promotional task designed to support lower-level room owners. Therefore, not all users will have access to this task.

  • Please note that eligibility is determined based on your room's performance during the previous week. Only room owners whose rooms received fewer than 400,000 diamonds in the previous week can receive this task.
