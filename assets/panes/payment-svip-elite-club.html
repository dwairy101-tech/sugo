The Elite Club is an exclusive membership for top rechargers. Here is how to qualify and join:

  • Qualification: Recharge a total of $1,500 USD or more within a single calendar month (from the 1st to the last day of the month).
  • Activation: Your membership will be automatically activated on the 1st day of the month following the month you qualified.

Example: If you recharge $1,500 between August 1 and August 31, your Elite Club membership starts on September 1.

  • Duration: Membership lasts for the entire calendar month. To maintain it, you need to qualify again the next month.

There is no separate application. Our system automatically tracks your recharges and grants membership when you meet the threshold.
