Dobi Coins are a special currency used in the Game Center. To check your balance and transaction history, follow these steps:

  • Go to the Game Center from the app's home screen or from the ME page if available.
  • Your Dobi Coin balance will be displayed on the main Game Center page.
  • To view your transaction history, tap the "Record" button in the top right corner. You will see a list of all Dobi Coin earnings and spendings.

Dobi Coins are separate from regular coins and diamonds. They are used for specific games and events.

Enjoy playing in the Game Center!

  • If you received a system notification showing that you have received Dobi Coins but are unsure where to check them, or you want to check your Dobi coin balance and record, please follow the steps below:
  • Check your Dobi Coin balance on the page.
  • Tap the “Record” button in the top right corner to view your Dobi Coin transaction history.
