Thank you for choosing to recharge on Sugo. Adding coins to your account is quick and easy. Please follow these steps:

  • Go to the "ME" page at the bottom right of the app.
  • Tap "Recharge".
  • You will see a list of coin packages with different amounts and prices. Choose the package you wish to purchase.
  • Select your preferred payment method. Available methods depend on your region and device (credit card, Google Play, Apple App Store, etc.).
  • Complete the payment through your app store or payment provider. Follow the on-screen instructions.
  • Once the payment is successful, the coins will be added to your account instantly. You can check your new balance on the ME page.

Please note: The currency displayed is based on the region of your Apple ID or Google Play account. To change currency, update your store region.

Thank you for supporting Sugo. Enjoy your coins!
