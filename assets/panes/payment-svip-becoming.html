Becoming an SVIP is straightforward. Please review the details below:

  • Your SVIP level is determined by your total recharge (in coins) over the last 30 days. Every 1 coin = 1 SVIP experience point.
  • There are 7 SVIP levels. The more you recharge, the higher your level.
  • To see your current level and how many more coins you need to reach the next level, go to ME → SVIP Center.
  • SVIP status is recalculated daily based on the rolling 30-day window.
  • Unlike VIP, SVIP does not have a permanent level; it can go up or down based on your recent recharge activity.

Keep recharging consistently to maintain or upgrade your SVIP status. Enjoy the exclusive benefits!
