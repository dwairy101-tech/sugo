Cancel Withdrawal

Answer

To cancel a withdrawal request, please specify the transaction you want to cancel so we can assist you correctly.

If the request is submitted within the first 24 hours of the transaction, it will be sent to management for review, and the diamonds may be returned within one week depending on the review result.

Please note that a withdrawal cannot be canceled after 24 hours have passed from the transaction time.

Welcome to Sugo,

To cancel a withdrawal request, please specify the transaction you want to cancel so we can assist you correctly.

If the request is submitted within the first 24 hours of the transaction, it will be sent to management for review, and the diamonds may be returned within one week depending on the review result.

Please note that a withdrawal cannot be canceled after 24 hours have passed from the transaction time.

We once again sincerely apologize for the inconvenience we have caused, and we truly thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a happy and successful day!

SUGO Customer Service Team

يرجى العلم أنه لا يمكن إلغاء عملية السحب بعد مرور 24 ساعة من تنفيذها.
