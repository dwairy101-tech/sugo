To help us review your phone binding request, please send your account ID, WhatsApp number, phone model, currently linked number/email, registration date, last active time, latest recharge screenshot, and the new phone number.

After receiving the complete information, the case will be forwarded to the binding team for review.

We once again sincerely apologize for the inconvenience we have caused, and we truly thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a happy and successful day!
