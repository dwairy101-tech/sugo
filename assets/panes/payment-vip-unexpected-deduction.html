Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

If you notice that your VIP points (store points) have decreased without an obvious reason, here is the explanation:

  • VIP points are valid for 90 days from the date they are earned.
  • When you redeem items from the point store, the system automatically uses the points that are closest to expiring first.
  • Therefore, a deduction may simply mean that some of your points expired or were used for a redemption.
  • To check your points history: Go to ME → VIP → tap the "Record" icon (top right). You will see all additions, redemptions, and expirations.

No points are ever deducted without a valid reason. Understanding this policy will help you keep track of your points.

  • نقاط VIP صالحة لمدة 90 يومًا من تاريخ كسبها.

لا يتم خصم أي نقاط أبدًا بدون سبب صالح. فهم هذه السياسة سيساعدك في تتبع نقاطك.
