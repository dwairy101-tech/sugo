Clash of Thrones is an idle cultivation + social mobile game set in a medieval low-magic world. Here is a quick overview:

  • Core loop: Open treasure chests → equip stronger gear → boost your combat power (CP).
  • Player role: You are a Lord. Build and upgrade your city and army.
  • Combat: 3x3 grid formation. You deploy soldiers, equipment, and weapons. Battles are large-scale army clashes (dozens of units per side).
  • Key stats: Health, Attack, Defense, Pierce, Morale, Attack Speed.
  • Progression: Idle/AFK friendly. You earn resources even when offline.
  • Social features: Form alliances, chat, and fight together.
  • Special features: Guardian Beast, Starlight currency, ladder rankings.

The game is designed for casual play. Log in daily to collect rewards and slowly grow your power.

  • Cultivation and Combat Systems
  • Player Role: You play as a Lord.
  • Core Progression: Build and upgrade your city and army.
  • Combat Setup: 3x3 grid formation using a card pool of soldiers, equipment, weapons, and more.
  • Battle Visuals: Epic large-scale army clashes with dozens of units per side, emphasizing group warfare over one-on-one duels.
  • Stats System: Streamlined to 6 essentials—Health, Attack, Defense, Pierce, Morale, and Attack Speed—for easy mastery.
  • Social Features: Alliances
  • Introduced at Level 5, once players grasp power-building basics.
  • Join an alliance for in-game chat and cross-alliance battles.
  • Chats sync automatically to SUGO group chats for seamless real-time coordination during fights.
  • Key Social Mode: Kingdom Clash (GVG)
  • A team-based Guild vs. Guild event where alliances compete as units.
  • Runs twice weekly (Wed/Sun) from 10:00 to 22:00 (12 hours per round).
  • Registration: Opens Mon/Fri at 00:00. Alliance leader signs up with at least 5 active members (logged in within 3 days). Prep phase starts Tue/Sat at 12:00.
  • Matchmaking: Alliances ranked by ELO score into tiers; same-tier groups enter one battlefield. Initial round is random seeding; later rounds use strength-based matching with dynamic K-factor adjustments for fair scoring.
  • In-Gameplay: City Conquest Players capture territories to earn "Kingdom Prestige." Each player can hold only 1 tile at a time. Empty tiles cost no combat uses; options include direct claim or battle conquest. Tiles have occupancy limits and protection periods.
  • Conquest Flags: 3 free at start (buy more with gold). Costs 1 to claim a tile; victorious battles only evict foes (no cost). Reset after each round.
  • Combat Uses: 8 free at start (regain 1 every 3 hours; buy extras with gold). Consumed per battle (win or lose); empty claims are free. Reset after each round.
  • Alliance Boost Cards: Up to 3 per alliance (doubles occupation rewards for all members). Each lasts 1 hour (extendable); purchasable in the final 30 minutes with alerts.
  • Per Round: Post-match, personal rewards based on battlefield and alliance rankings within your zone.
  • Season-End: Alliance honors by overall ranking; personal rewards by zone performance. Kingdom Prestige converts 1:1 to alliance currency.

Clash of Thrones هي لعبة تكوينية غير نشطة + اجتماعية على الأجهزة المحمولة تدور أحداثها في عالم منخفض السحر في العصور الوسطى. إليك نظرة عامة سريعة:

  • الحلقة الأساسية: افتح صناديق الكنز ← جهّز معدات أقوى ← عزز قوتك القتالية.
  • دور اللاعب: أنت لورد. قم ببناء وتطوير مدينتك وجيشك.
  • القتال: تشكيل شبكي 3x3. تنشر الجنود والمعدات والأسلحة. المعارك هي اشتباكات واسعة النطاق للجيوش (عشرات الوحدات لكل جانب).
  • الإحصائيات الرئيسية: الصحة، الهجوم، الدفاع، الاختراق، الروح، سرعة الهجوم.
  • التقدم: صديق للعب غير النشط. تكسب الموارد حتى عندما تكون غير متصل.
  • الميزات الاجتماعية: تشكيل تحالفات ودردشة والقتال معًا.
  • ميزات خاصة: الحامي، عملة Starlight، التصنيفات السلمية.

اللعبة مصممة للعب غير الرسمي. قم بتسجيل الدخول يوميًا لجمع المكافآت وتنمية قوتك ببطء.
