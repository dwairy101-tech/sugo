To view your complete coin transaction history, follow these simple steps:

  • Go to the "ME" page.
  • Tap "Recharge".
  • Tap the record icon in the top right corner (usually looks like a clock or list).
  • You will see a detailed list of all your coin additions and deductions, including dates, amounts, and reasons for each transaction.
  • You can scroll through the history to review past activity.

This feature helps you track your spending and verify that all transactions are correct.
