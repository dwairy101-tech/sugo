The in-game chat in Clash of Thrones allows you to communicate with other players. Here are the available channels and rules:

World chat: Public channel where all players can talk. Use it to ask questions, find allies, or chat casually.

Alliance chat: Private channel for your alliance members only. Best for coordination and strategy.

Private messages: Direct messages to individual players. Tap on a player's name or avatar to send a private message.

System messages: Automated messages about events, battles, alliance updates, and rewards.

Chat rules:

  • No spamming, harassment, hate speech, or inappropriate language.
  • No advertising other games or services.
  • No sharing of personal information.

Violations may lead to chat restrictions, temporary mute, or account bans.

Language: Most players use English or their local language. Be respectful of others.

Emojis and stickers: You can use standard emojis. Some custom stickers are available through events.

Use chat wisely to build friendships and coordinate effectively.

Sugo Support Team
