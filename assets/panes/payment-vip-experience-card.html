A VIP Experience Card is a special item that boosts the VIP EXP you earn from recharging by a certain percentage.

  • How to get: You can earn these cards from events such as Lucky Adventure, Elite Club, and other promotions.
  • How to use: Go to your Backpack, find the VIP Experience Card, and tap "Activate". The card's validity period starts immediately.
  • Recharge: While the card is active, any recharge you make will earn bonus VIP EXP according to the card's percentage.
  • Duration: Each card has a validity period (e.g., 3 days, 7 days). After it expires, the boost stops.

⚠️ Important: VIP Experience Cards do NOT affect SVIP EXP. They only boost VIP EXP.

Use these cards strategically when you plan to recharge large amounts to maximize your VIP growth.

  • A VIP Experience Card is a special reward item that gives you a percentage-based boost on all VIP Experience Points (EXP) you gain from recharging.
  • You can earn these cards as rewards in various activities, such as the Lucky Adventure, the Elite Club, and other special events. Each card clearly shows its unique boost percentage and how long it is valid for.
  • How to Use Your VIP Experience Card
  • Using your card is a simple, two-step process:
  • Find and Activate Your Card: Go to your "Backpack" to see your collected cards. Tap on the one you want to use and select "Activate". The card's validity period begins immediately.
  • Recharge Your Account: Make any recharge purchase while the card is active. The EXP boost will be applied automatically to all VIP experience points you gain during the card's validity period.
  • Will the VIP Experience Card also boost my SVIP experience points?
  • No, unfortunately not. The card is designed to boost VIP experience points only and does not apply to the SVIP system.

بطاقة خبرة VIP هي عنصر خاص يزيد من نقاط خبرة VIP التي تكسبها من الشحن بنسبة مئوية معينة.

  • كيفية الحصول: يمكنك كسب هذه البطاقات من الفعاليات مثل المغامرة المحظوظة ونادي النخبة والعروض الأخرى.
  • كيفية الاستخدام: اذهب إلى حقيبة الظهر، وابحث عن بطاقة خبرة VIP، واضغط على "تفعيل". تبدأ فترة صلاحية البطاقة فورًا.
  • الشحن: أثناء تفعيل البطاقة، أي عملية شحن تقوم بها ستمنحك مكافأة إضافية من نقاط خبرة VIP وفقًا لنسبة البطاقة.
  • المدة: كل بطاقة لها فترة صلاحية (مثال: 3 أيام، 7 أيام). بعد انتهاء صلاحيتها، يتوقف التعزيز.

⚠️ مهم: بطاقات خبرة VIP لا تؤثر على نقاط خبرة SVIP. تعزز فقط نقاط خبرة VIP.

استخدم هذه البطاقات بشكل استراتيجي عندما تخطط لشحن مبالغ كبيرة لتعظيم نمو VIP الخاص بك.
