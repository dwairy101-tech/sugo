Welcome to the SUGO family!

We sincerely apologize for the issue you are experiencing.

We want to assure you about your payment security. Please understand the following:

  • Sugo does not store your credit card or payment details on our servers. Your payment information is handled entirely by your app store (Google Play or Apple App Store).
  • When you make a purchase, the transaction is processed by Google or Apple, not by Sugo.
  • To remove saved payment details, you need to manage them through your Google Account or Apple ID settings, not within Sugo.

For Google: Go to payments.google.com or your Google Play settings to remove saved cards.

For Apple: Go to Settings → [your name] → Payment & Shipping. From there, you can remove or update your payment methods.

No payment information is stored inside the Sugo app itself.

Your financial security is our priority. If you have further questions, please refer to Google or Apple support.

Sugo Support Team
