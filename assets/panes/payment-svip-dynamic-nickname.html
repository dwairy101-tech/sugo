A dynamic (animated) nickname makes your name stand out with moving effects. Here is how to get one:

  • Go to ME → VIP/SVIP Center → SVIP Store.
  • Look for the "Dynamic Nickname" item. It is available for exchange using SVIP points.

Dynamic nicknames are visible to all users on your profile, in chats, and in rooms.
