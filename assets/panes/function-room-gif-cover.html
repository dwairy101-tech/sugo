You can make your room stand out by setting an animated GIF as the room cover photo. Here are the requirements and steps:

Requirements:

  • Latest version of the Sugo app.
  • Your room must be at least Level 12.
  • You need enough coins to pay for the GIF cover (cost varies by duration).

Steps:

  • Enter your room.
  • Tap "..." (top right) → "Room Settings" → "Room Cover Photo".
  • Select "GIF Cover" from the options.
  • Choose a GIF from your device gallery or select a preloaded one (if available).
  • Choose a validity period: 3 days, 7 days, or 30 days. The cost increases with duration.
  • Confirm and pay the required coins.

After setting: The GIF cover will be visible to everyone who enters your room. It will animate automatically.

Expiration: When the validity period ends, the cover reverts to the default or your previous static cover.

Renewal: You can renew by repeating the process before it expires.

GIF covers attract more attention to your room. Use them for special events or to celebrate milestones.

المتطلبات:
