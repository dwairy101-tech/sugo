If you want to create your own agency (as a leader), you must meet specific requirements and follow a formal process.

Requirement: You must have a team of at least 5 new anchors ready to join your agency immediately upon approval.

Anchor criteria:

  • Each anchor must have a new account (within 14 days of creation).
  • One account per device.
  • No existing host accounts on the same device.

Application process:

  • Fill out the official Agency Creation Request Form (available through support).
  • Provide the list of anchor account IDs and their verification proofs.
  • Submit the application.

Review: Our Agency Support team will review your application within 24 hours and contact you via WhatsApp.

Contact for assistance: WhatsApp +20 1070449100 (Agency Support).

Note: As a CS agent, you should not just send the link to users. You must collect all required information from the user and submit the application on their behalf using the designated form.

Creating an agency is a big responsibility. Ensure you have committed anchors.
